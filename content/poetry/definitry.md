**definetry** /dɛfɪnɛtʃʰɹi/\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The art of defining\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A word without rhyming.\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;(Not even a bit\
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For style nor wit)
